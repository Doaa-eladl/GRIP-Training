Bank History

We are proud to partner with the Platinum Jubilee Pageant. For over 250 years we have supported the people and businesses of Britain and Her Majesty has reigned for 70 of those years. The Pageant is a way to celebrate her lifetime of commitment and service. But also to look forward to a future that sees Britain continue to prosper in ever more inclusive and sustainable ways. .
